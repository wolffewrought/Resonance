📲 Install Resonance

Add Resonance to your Home Screen for full offline access and no browser bar.

iPhone / iPad (Safari)
1
Make sure you're in Safari — this won't work in Chrome or Firefox
2
Tap the Share icon □↑ in the toolbar (bottom on iPhone, top on iPad)
3
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4
Tap Add — Resonance appears on your Home Screen
Android / Chrome: Tap ⋮ menu → "Add to Home screen" or "Install app". If you don't see it, Chrome may show an install icon in the address bar.
